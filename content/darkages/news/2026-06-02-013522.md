# You awaken in the Mileth Inn

The walk out of the tutorial now ends somewhere warmer. Finish your training and you wake up inside the Mileth Inn proper, not on the street outside it, with Riona the innkeeper right there to greet you.

![Riona, the Mileth innkeeper](/darkages/images/riona.png)

## A proper welcome

Speak with her and she sets you on your way. She tells you that you have left your mundane life behind and become an Aisling, free to make your own path, and she points you toward finding a guide and seeing the world. For the trouble of saying hello she hands you five hundred experience to start with.

## Your mundane birth

She also keeps a small ritual. Ask her about your mundane birth and she walks you through it: the town you spent your mundane years in, and the sun and moon you were born under. Answer and she writes it into your legend for good, a short line of who you were before the spark took you. As she warns you, once it is set it is not meant to be undone.

## One Riona, in the right place

A quick note on names: Riona is the innkeeper of Mileth, full stop. We had briefly hung her name on the wrong shop, and that is fixed, so there is one Riona in town and she is exactly where she should be.

This is a quiet feature, but it is the kind of quiet that matters. It is the first face you meet once training is over, and getting that handshake right sets the tone for everything after. Next we keep working down the Mileth list: the townsfolk who should have their own things to say, the first quests, and the path that leads down into the crypt.
