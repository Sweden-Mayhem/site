# Play

Everything you need to get into Sweden Mayhem Dark Ages.

---

## Download

The client is a single folder. No install and nothing to configure. It already knows where to find us.

<a href="https://github.com/Sweden-Mayhem/darkages-client/releases/latest/download/SwedenMayhem-Client-Win.zip" class="button">Download for Windows</a>

It is a large download, around 600 MB, because all of the game art ships inside it.

---

## Getting in

1. Unzip the folder anywhere you like
2. Open **Chaos.Client.exe**
3. Make a character, or log in if you already have one
4. You wake up in the Mileth Inn. Welcome back.

The client connects to our server on its own. There is nothing to type in.

---

## Your first steps

- **Fight the mice** in the inn. Spacebar swings your fists.
- **Walk into Mileth.** Talk to the shopkeepers and poke around the buildings.
- **Find the crypt** in the northeast corner of town. The stairs go down, and so does your survival rate.
- **Spend your gold.** Shops sell gear and potions, and banks hold what you cannot carry.

---

## Good to know

- Your character lives on our server, so your progress is safe between sessions.
- The world is the old Dark Ages map, rebuilt. If a door or a warp looks wrong, tell us.
- These are early days. Expect rough edges, and expect them to get smoother.
