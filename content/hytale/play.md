## Joining the server

1. Launch Hytale
2. Click **Servers**
3. Click **Add Server** (in the corner)
4. For **Connection Address**, enter:  
    `hytale.swedenmayhem.se`
5. For **Name**, enter:  
    `Sweden Mayhem`
6. Double-click the server entry to join!

---

## Your first 15 minutes

When you first join, you spawn in the central hub area. Here is what to do:

- **Look around spawn.** There are merchants, portals, and signs that explain the basics. Take a minute to read them.
- **Claim land early.** Use the claim system to protect your base. Unclaimed builds outside claims can be wiped during the weekly Friday reset.
- **Check the map.** Press **M** to open the world map. It shows claimed areas and points of interest.
- **Grab a weapon.** Head into the wild, gather materials, and craft your first gear. Enemies start easy but get tougher as you explore further out.
- **Open the menu.** Press **T** and type `/menu` to see your skills, stats, and available commands.

---

## Key systems to know

**Skill Trees.** Combat, crafting, and gathering all have RPG progression. Kill enemies, mine ores, chop trees, and you will level up those skills over time. Higher levels unlock access to better gear and content.

**Instanced Dungeons.** Portals around the world take you into private dungeon instances. Loot is per-player, so you do not compete with others for drops.

**Weekly Wilderness Reset.** Every Friday morning, the wilderness outside claimed land resets. Resources refresh, structures regrow, and the world gets a clean slate. Your claimed builds are always safe.

**Dark Coins.** The endgame currency. Earned from tough content, spent on rare items and repairs. Hold on to them.

**Merchants.** NPCs sell useful items for various currencies. Explore to find them all.

**Tickets.** Found a bug or need help? Type `/ticket create your message here` in chat. Staff will get back to you.

## Useful commands

| Command | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| `/menu` | Opens the main menu (skills, stats, settings) |
| `/ticket create [message]` | Report a bug or ask for help |
| `/claim` | Open the land claim interface |
| `/portal` | Manage your personal portals |
| `/voice join` | Join proximity voice chat |
| `/trade` | Open the player trade market |
| `/vote` | Get the link to vote for the server |

---

## Tips from regulars

- Do not rush to endgame. The progression is designed to feel rewarding at every stage.
- Explore beyond the safe zone. Better resources and dungeon entrances are further out.
- The Friday reset is your friend. It brings back resources without touching your base.
- If something feels broken, file a ticket. We fix things fast.
