# Play

Ready to join? Here is everything you need to know to get onto Sweden Mayhem.

---

## Requirements

We are almost always up to date with the **latest stable release** of Hytale. No client-side mods are required - if you have the base game, you are good to go.

If you do not own Hytale yet, you can buy it on the official store:

[Buy Hytale »](https://store.hytale.com)

---

## Joining the server

1. Launch Hytale
2. Click **Servers**
3. Click **Add Server** (in the corner)
4. For **Connection Address**, enter:  
    `hytale.swedenmayhem.se`
5. For **Name**, enter:  
    `Sweden Mayhem`
6. Double-click the server entry to join!

---

That is it. See you in-world.
