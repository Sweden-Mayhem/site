# Service Update

Update <span style="color:#55FF55">1.7.64</span> brings menu improvements and shortcuts!

<span style="color:#FFFF55">User Interface</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Information Board:</span>
      - New Shortcuts page added to the Information Board so you can keep quick access to favorite locations and pages
      - You can enable or disable the Shortcuts page in your personal menu settings
      - Rename your Shortcuts page and set it to open as the first tab when you open the Information Board
      - Option to open the Information Board using the walk (Alt) key
      - Visual refresh and layout improvements for the Information Board
      - Various small fixes for a smoother experience

Note - Shortcuts page is new and may have minor issues
