# Service Update

Version <span style="color:#55FF55">1.8.22</span> brings quality-of-life updates, fixes, and a few small improvements!

<span style="color:#FFFF55">New</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Combat:</span>
      - Incoming-attack visual and sound cue added (BETA) so you get a clear hint when an enemy is about to hit you
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Information Board:</span>
      - The Information Board and in-game menus are now available in 9 languages; your language choice applies across menus

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Bug Fixes</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Shops & Auctions:</span>
      - Fixed an issue where items you sold to player shops sometimes stayed in your chest
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Enemies & Loot:</span>
      - Fixed rare cases where enemies failed to load correctly or dropped the wrong loot
      - Removed stray debug labels that sometimes appeared on enemies
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Combat:</span>
      - Parry now works correctly with enchantments so defensive timing behaves as expected
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">HUD & Nameplates:</span>
      - HUD layout now saves correctly for new players
      - The "what you're looking at" info HUD now works properly alongside other HUDs
      - Nameplates no longer stick to dead creatures and are removed when players leave the area

<span style="color:#FFFF55">User Interface</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Item Browser:</span>
      - Item lookup tool now has cleaner, easier-to-read tooltips

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Performance</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Nameplates:</span>
      - Nameplates are now skipped for entities with no nearby players, reducing lag in busy areas

Note: The new incoming-attack VFX/SFX is marked BETA and may be tweaked further.

Thanks to <span style="color:#55FFFF">narwhals, KyuubiDDragon, makapar, Frotty277, Lullerooz, DarkhaxDev, Buuz135</span> for the updates!
