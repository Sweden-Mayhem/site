## What Changed in the Skill System

Four updates landed between Tuesday and Thursday. Here's what actually changed:

**Achievements are live.** The skill screen now has an achievements tab. Around 200 milestones across combat, gathering, crafting, and exploration, plus auto-generated five-tier chains for every skill. They track silently as you play. Hitting certain point totals earns rewards, and server-first completions get a special badge.

**Acrobatics works differently now.** It used to mainly train by taking fall damage. Now it trains through movement - sprinting, swimming, and surviving big drops. If your fall reduction hits 100% you take zero damage and earn a bigger XP bonus. If you were grinding it the old way, the new method is both safer and more varied.

**Combo finishers in melee.** Skill trees now include bonuses that deal extra damage on finisher hits. A notification fires when one lands. Worth checking your tree if you fight a lot.

**A quiet but important XP fix.** Skill tree bonuses like "+3% Mining XP" were silently applying to every skill at once instead of just the one they were claimed under. If you had stacked several bonuses across multiple skills, your gains may have been running higher than intended. It's fixed now - each bonus applies to its own skill only. Existing unlocks were automatically corrected.
