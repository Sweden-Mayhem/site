# Service Update

Version <span style="color:#55FF55">1.7.56</span> brings parry improvements, stamina returns, and a new counter-attack reticle!

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Gameplay</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Combat:</span>
      - Perfect parries now restore a portion of your stamina when timed correctly
      - A counter-attack reticle has been added to help time follow-up strikes after a parry
      - Parry timing now differs between PvP and PvE: timing is tighter in PvP and slightly more forgiving in PvE

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Bug Fixes</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Combat fixes:</span>
      - Various combat fixes and optimizations to make parries and stamina behave more reliably

Note: the counter-attack reticle and stamina-return parry are new and may still have a few rough edges

Thanks to <span style="color:#55FFFF">narwhals</span> for the improvements!
