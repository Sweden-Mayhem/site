# Service Update

Version <span style="color:#55FF55">1.7.49</span> is live - merchants got bolder and their wares improved!

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Gameplay</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Wandering Merchants:</span>
      - Wandering merchants are more persistent now - they may follow you around and stare until you interact with them.
      - Merchant companions are more active nearby and will step in to assist or protect players.
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Merchandise:</span>
      - Merchants can now sometimes offer higher-tier equipment in their rotating stock. These items appear in the merchant trade slots and cost 1 or 2 emeralds.

Note: The new high-tier gear is on a rotation and may be rare while it settles in.

Thanks to <span style="color:#55FFFF">MightyMort</span> for the improvements!
