# Service Update

Update <span style="color:#55FF55">1.7.40</span> brings combat improvements and fixes!

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Gameplay</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Combat:</span>
      - Counterattacks now register reliably even against long charged attacks. Timing is based on your click, so parrying feels more consistent.
      - Stuns from repeated hits interact better with enchantments and other combat effects, so chain stuns play more smoothly.

   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Audio:</span>
      - New sound effects for successful parries, counterattacks, and stuns have been added.

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Bug Fixes</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">PvP:</span>
      - Stamina drains in PvP now calculate correctly, so stamina costs behave as expected during fights.

   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Stamina Display:</span>
      - The stamina readout no longer updates constantly and should stop causing lag; stamina updates feel smoother.

Note: The new combat sounds are live now and may have small issues as we fine-tune them.

Thanks to <span style="color:#55FFFF">narwhals</span> for these improvements!
