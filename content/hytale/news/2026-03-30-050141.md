# Service Update

Version <span style="color:#55FF55">1.8.1</span> brings improvements to nameplates and stability fixes!

<span style="color:#FFFF55">New</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Nameplates:</span>
      - NPCs now display their in-world names (for example "Archaeopteryx")
      - You can toggle nameplates on or off for each world and instance
      - Toggle whole groups of nameplate segments from a single source on or off
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Controls & Preview:</span>
      - Reorder which parts of a nameplate are shown and preview how they look on different creature types
      - Nameplate text can now show viewer-specific info (personalized placeholders)

<span style="color:#FFFF55">User Interface</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Settings UI:</span>
      - Nameplate settings are split into Ordering and Settings tabs to make controls clearer
      - The preview bar shows how nameplates look for different entity types
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Locked Options:</span>
      - If a setting is disabled by staff it will show as \"(Disabled by Admin)\" and cannot be changed

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Bug Fixes</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Stability:</span>
      - Fixed crashes that could happen when certain anchored entities left loaded areas
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Nameplates:</span>
      - Fixed cases where some NPC nameplates were being overwritten or not showing correctly

Some of the new nameplate controls are fresh and may be tweaked further.

Thanks to <span style="color:#55FFFF">Frotty277</span> for the improvements!
