Version <span style="color:#55FF55">1.8.22</span> brings fresh fixes and updates!

<span style="color:#FFFF55">New</span>

      - Skill and combat HUD layout now saves correctly for new players
      - Nameplates are more efficient and now clean up correctly for dead or distant entities, reducing lag

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Other Changes</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Server Notes:</span>
