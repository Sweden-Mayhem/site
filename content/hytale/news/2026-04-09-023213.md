# Service Update

Version <span style="color:#55FF55">1.8.25</span> brings smoother, clearer nameplates and a nicer editor experience!



<span style="color:#FFFF55">User Interface</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Nameplates:</span>
      - Reorder nameplate segments by dragging and dropping
      - Vertical offset adjusted with an easy slider
      - Tooltips added to buttons and controls for clearer options
      - Scrollbars now have a gold tint for better visibility
      - Save button is greyed out when there are no unsaved changes
      - Editor shows more items in the available, disabled, and blacklist sections
      - Editor displays its version in the title bar
      - Fixed a typo in the separator editor



<span style="color:#FFFF55">Quality of Life</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Nameplates:</span>
      - Nameplate preferences save more reliably and old settings are migrated automatically
      - Some nameplate pieces may be enabled by default for new players



<span style="color:#FFFF55">Gameplay</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Nameplates:</span>
      - Nameplates should perform better and feel smoother when many players are nearby

Note: Drag-and-drop ordering is a new feature and may have minor issues.

Thanks to <span style="color:#55FFFF">Frotty277</span> for the improvements!
