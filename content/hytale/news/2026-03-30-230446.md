# Service Update

Version <span style="color:#55FF55">1.8.4</span> is live - small fixes and a few quality of life updates!

<span style="color:#FFFF55">New</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">NPCs:</span>
      - NPCs now show their names directly on their nameplates

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Gameplay</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Enemies:</span>
      - RPG-style enemies have been re-enabled and updated - expect tiered mobs with varied gear and improved loot

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Bug Fixes</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Stability:</span>
      - Fixed crashes that could happen when NPCs were given certain gear
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">HUD:</span>
      - Fixed an issue where the skill and combat HUD did not always refresh correctly

<span style="color:#FFFF55">User Interface</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Nameplates:</span>
      - You can toggle nameplates on or off per world and instance, and preview how they look on different mob types
      - If a setting is disabled by staff you will see \"(Disabled by Admin)\" so you know why it cannot be changed
      - Fixed cases where nameplates would disappear or be overwritten

Some new things to try: NPC names on nameplates and the re-enabled RPG-style enemies - they are new/updated and may have a few rough edges.

Thanks to <span style="color:#55FFFF">Frotty277</span> and <span style="color:#55FFFF">Lullerooz</span> for the improvements!
