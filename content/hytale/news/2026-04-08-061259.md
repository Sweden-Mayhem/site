# Service Update

Version <span style="color:#55FF55">1.8.22</span> is live!

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Gameplay</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Combat:</span>
      - Perfect parry now works correctly with enchanted gear, and a BETA visual/audio cue was added to warn when an attack is incoming
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Shops:</span>
      - Player shops properly remove sold items from their shop chests again
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Mobs:</span>
      - Several enemy variants and their loot now load and appear correctly, so some previously missing or broken mob types should behave as expected

<span style="color:#FFFF55">User Interface</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">HUDs:</span>
      - Skill/combat HUD layout now saves correctly for new players
      - The "look at" info HUD now works together with other HUDs without conflicts
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Nameplates:</span>
      - Nameplates no longer stick to dead entities and clean up when players leave the area, improving clarity and performance around lots of mobs
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Item Info:</span>
      - The item browser tooltips have an improved, cleaner design
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Menus & Language:</span>
      - The Information Board and other menus now respect your selected language (multi-language support added)

Note: the incoming-attack visual/audio cue is BETA and may have rough edges.
