# Service Update

Version <span style="color:#55FF55">1.7.59</span> brings dungeon updates and quality-of-life changes!

<span style="color:#FFFF55">New</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Localization:</span>
      - Dungeons, enemies and related text are now available in Russian, Portuguese (Portugal), Spanish (Spain), French and Hungarian

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Bug Fixes</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Bosses:</span>
      - Boss health bar has been temporarily disabled and will be restored in a future update

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Gameplay</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Loot:</span>
      - Cult armor no longer drops from regular Cult Knights - it now only drops from Bosses and Mini-Bosses
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Salvaging:</span>
      - You can now recycle any armor at the Salvager's Workbench

Thanks to <span style="color:#55FFFF">MAJOR76</span> for the improvements!
