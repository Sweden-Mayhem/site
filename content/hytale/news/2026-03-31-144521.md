# Service Update

Version <span style="color:#55FF55">1.8.6</span> brings improvements to shops and buyers!

<span style="color:#FFFF55">New</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Shops:</span>
      - You can now sell items at NPC shops using BUY and SELL tabs
      - New buyer NPCs have been placed around town to buy items from players

<span style="color:#FFFF55">User Interface</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Shops UI:</span>
      - Selling now uses a confirmation dialog with a quantity selector and shows the total price
      - Daily sell limits are shown so you know how many of an item you can sell each day

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Gameplay</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Economy:</span>
      - Some items cannot be sold at shops, so check the SELL tab before trying to sell
      - Shops show either a fixed sell price per item or calculate the price as a percentage
      - Some shops may use special currencies when buying items

Selling at NPC shops is a new feature and may have occasional hiccups as we fine-tune it.

Thanks to <span style="color:#55FFFF">KyuubiDDragon</span> for the improvements!
