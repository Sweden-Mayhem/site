# Service Update

Version <span style="color:#55FF55">1.8.14</span> brings language support, fixes, and quality-of-life tweaks!

<span style="color:#FFFF55">User Interface</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Information Board & Menus:</span>
      - The Information Board and other server menus are now available in 9 languages; switching language updates all server menus immediately and fixes pages that sometimes stayed in the wrong language.
      - Help, Rules, Server Info, Team and Search pages now follow your chosen language.
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Nameplates:</span>
      - Nameplates are simplified by default to show name and health, previews and scrolling work better, and NPCs now get nameplates correctly.

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Gameplay</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Mobs & Loot:</span>
      - Elite enemies now drop normal vanilla loot again (items like fabric scraps and bone fragments) when defeated.

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Shops</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Player Shops:</span>
      - Player-run barter shops no longer leave sold items behind in chests; items are removed correctly when sold.

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Events</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Comet Raids:</span>
      - Most comet raid themes now spawn naturally, so you may see more random comet events.
      - Reward chests from portals and coffins are placed more reliably in front of the asset and now face north for easier access.
      - You can no longer accidentally restart a raid while its reward chest still exists.

Note: Multi-language support is new and may have minor issues; please report anything odd.
