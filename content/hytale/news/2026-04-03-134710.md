# Service Update

Update <span style="color:#55FF55">1.8.16</span> brings fixes, quality-of-life tweaks, and a small BETA visual for combat!

<span style="color:#FFFF55">New</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Combat:</span>
      - A new BETA visual and sound cue for perfect parries has been added so you get clearer feedback when an attack is incoming. It may still be rough around the edges.
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Information Board & UI:</span>
      - The Information Board and its pages (Help, Rules, Server Info, Team, Search) now support multiple languages and follow your selected language.
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Skill HUD:</span>
      - Skill HUD now shows progress bars so you can watch skill progress in real time.
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Books & Papers:</span>
      - A lectern was added so you can showcase and copy books more easily.

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Bug Fixes</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">NPCs:</span>
      - Bugmen Merchant and Fogmen Worker NPCs were fixed and should behave correctly now instead of getting stuck or acting odd.
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Items & Books:</span>
      - Parchment items and interactions have been fixed so they work correctly.
      - Book and parchment sounds were restored so reading and writing feel right again.
      - Fixed a crash that could happen when your inventory was full while interacting with the books system.
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Shops & Marketplace:</span>
      - Player-run barter shops no longer leave sold items behind in chests; selling now correctly removes the item.

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Gameplay</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Creature Loot:</span>
      - Monster drops were cleaned up: equipment no longer drops from mobs and any unobtainable materials were removed from loot so drops match what players can actually use.
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">World Events:</span>
      - An unstable world event that could cause instability was disabled to keep the server stable.

<span style="color:#FFFF55">User Interface</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Nameplates & NPC Picker:</span>
      - Nameplate visuals and the NPC selection UI were improved. NPC selection now picks the correct NPC when filters are used, and pagination/labels are clearer.

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Other Changes</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Assets & Stability:</span>
      - Missing sound assets were restored for books and papers.
      - Various behind-the-scenes fixes were applied to get things working smoothly across the server.
