# Sweden Mayhem Weekly - Issue #17, 2026

**Quietly leveling up.**

---

## Evertian on a Roll

Monday evening was busy for Evertian. In the space of about 20 minutes, they hit level 85 in Harvesting, tore through Crafting from 60 to 65, and capped off the night at level 70 in Archery. That puts their total level well past 500 - a serious milestone for any server.

If you want to see what top-level play looks like on Sweden Mayhem, Evertian is it.

---

## Some New Faces

Seven players stopped by this week. Three came on Tuesday - two in the afternoon and Flamunda late at night. Then Eleganten420 appeared Thursday morning, poked around for about half an hour, and even found their way into the Forgotten Temple. Brief visit, but they clearly knew what they were doing. ---

## Server Updates

Version 1.8.30 landed mid-week. The Connected Windows mod was updated - windows and glass panes placed next to each other now connect their textures more reliably. Small thing, but if you build with windows you'll notice it.

Version 1.8.31 followed early Friday morning and 1.8.32 came out Saturday morning - both routine maintenance updates with no player-facing changes.

Version 1.8.33 arrived early Sunday. The skill progression system was updated with a handful of fixes, the most notable being that elite mobs now clean up properly when they die - no more floating nameplates after a kill. If you noticed ghost labels hanging around after elite encounters, that should be gone now.

Version 1.8.34 came out Sunday evening - land claims and a couple of other systems were updated in the background. Nothing player-facing changed.

---

## Volt Glider - Still Waiting

No change on the Lightning Essence front. The ingredient is not yet available in-game. We will post an announcement on the Information Board when that changes.
