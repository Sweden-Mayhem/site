# Service Update

Version <span style="color:#55FF55">1.8.9</span> brings fixes and quality-of-life improvements!

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Bug Fixes</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">NPCs:</span>
      - Mort's Wandering Merchant now loads correctly and its Bugmen and Fogmen appear as intended
      - Reduced friendly fire damage between elite NPCs so they fight each other less often (they may still attack after accidental hits)
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Items & Crafting:</span>
      - Crafting recipe for the Red Wall Torch restored to the working version
      - Parchment item fixed so interactions and sounds work correctly
      - Asset problems affecting the Red Wall Torch and Parchment have been resolved so they appear and behave properly
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Loot:</span>
      - Mobs will now always drop their vanilla loot correctly, even in cases that previously prevented those drops

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Gameplay</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Comet Raids:</span>
      - Most comet raid themes now spawn naturally without needing manual start
      - Reward chests are placed more reliably in front of portals/coffins to avoid overlap, and you cannot retrigger a wave while its reward chest still exists

<span style="color:#FFFF55">User Interface</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Nameplates:</span>
      - Nameplates now display built-in info like health and names correctly for NPCs and players
      - Vanilla NPCs now reliably get nameplates regardless of entity type

Comet raid natural spawning is now enabled for most themes; keep an eye out as we continue to tune it.

Thanks to <span style="color:#55FFFF">NitradoAndre</span>, <span style="color:#55FFFF">metrakit</span>, <span style="color:#55FFFF">Frotty277</span>, and <span style="color:#55FFFF">Project42</span> for the improvements!
