# Service Update

Version <span style="color:#55FF55">1.8.19</span> is live! Small improvements, stability fixes, and a few new QoL additions.

<span style="color:#FFFF55">New</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">User Interface:</span>
      - The Information Board and all in-game menus now support 9 languages; your chosen language is applied across menus and NPC text
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">HUD:</span>
      - Skill HUD now shows progress bars so you can see skill progress in real time
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Books:</span>
      - Lecterns can now showcase and copy books
      - You can send empty books if you want to (yes, really)

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Gameplay</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Combat:</span>
      - Parry timing now works better with enchantments and there is a new BETA visual and sound cue when an attack is incoming (may be rough while in BETA)
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Shops & Economy:</span>
      - Player-run barter shops no longer leave sold items behind in shop chests after a sale
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Mobs:</span>
      - Enemy variety and loot loading issues have been fixed so fewer enemy types fail to appear or have missing drops

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Bug Fixes</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Crashes & Stability:</span>
      - Fixed crashes related to nameplates and book/mail interactions, including a crash when sending mail with a full inventory
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Text & Nameplates:</span>
      - Fixed text display problems in nameplates so names and formatting show correctly
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Crafting & Mail:</span>
      - Missing mailbox recipe restored and mail tooltips clarified

Thanks to <span style="color:#55FFFF">narwhals, KyuubiDDragon, makapar, Frotty277, Lullerooz, Conczin</span> for the improvements!
