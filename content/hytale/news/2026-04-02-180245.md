# Service Update

Update <span style="color:#55FF55">1.8.11</span> is live! Small improvements to languages, shops, raids, nameplates and enemy loot.

<span style="color:#FFFF55">New</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Languages:</span>
      - The Information Board and all server menus now support 9 languages; your chosen language is applied across Help, Rules, Server Info, Team and search pages, and NPC text.
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Comet Raids:</span>
      - Most comet raid themes will now appear naturally in the world by default; a few special themes remain non-natural.

<span style="color:#FFFF55">User Interface</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Information Board:</span>
      - Language selection now applies instantly across the Information Board and related UI pages.
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Nameplates:</span>
      - Nameplates for NPCs and monsters now display correctly for all types and show health and name segments more reliably.

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Gameplay</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Barter Shops:</span>
      - Player-run Barter Shops now correctly remove sold items from their chests.
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Comet Raids:</span>
      - Reward chests for comet events are now placed reliably in front of portals/coffins, face away from the asset, and avoid overlapping.
      - Interacting with an active comet block will no longer trigger a new wave while its reward chest still exists.
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Enemies & Loot:</span>
      - Enemy loot now reliably includes vanilla drops when applicable.
      - Enemy health scaling now applies correctly after restarts.
      - Elite allies deal less friendly fire damage to each other.
