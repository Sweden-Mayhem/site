# Sweden Mayhem Weekly - Issue #16, 2026

**Updates, fixes, and a parry that finally lands.**

---

## This Week's Updates

Several server restarts this week, but none of them emergencies - each one applied a new round of improvements.

The biggest one: the parry system got a full pass. Counterattack windows now trigger reliably (follow-up hits were sometimes not connecting), parry sound effects play correctly across all weapons, and the parry reticle now updates in real time so you can time perfect parries by feel rather than guessing. If combat has felt inconsistent lately, this is the fix.

Also landed this week: nameplate improvements (NPCs no longer show suffixes like "Warden" or "Patrol" - just the clean name), better stability for NPC shop interactions and loot bags, and chat now supports multiple prefixes at once.

---

## Boost Confirmed

The Grand (10x, 45 min) and Epic (20x, 60 min) XP Boost Tokens added last week are confirmed working. Both are available at the same place as the original 5x token - the Dark Coin Shop.

Reminder: boosts don't stack. If you pop a Grand while a Major is still active, only the higher multiplier applies.

## Evertian's Week

Level 500 total by end of Sunday. Crafting hit 55 on Saturday, then Archery jumped from 60 to 65 in a single Sunday evening session - the last push came during a Dungeon of Fear III run. Harvesting still leads at 83, Acrobatics at 68, Archery now at 65.

There was one incident mid-week: leaving the Weekly World instance while it reset sent the character to the wrong world. Took a couple of minutes to sort out. Worth knowing - try to exit the Weekly World before it resets rather than letting the timer push you out.
