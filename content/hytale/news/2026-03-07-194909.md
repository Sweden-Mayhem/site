# Service Update

Version <span style="color:#55FF55">1.7.51</span> brings new dangers and rewards!

<span style="color:#FFFF55">New</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Dungeons & Bosses:</span>
      - Baron Silver General added - a werewolf former knight as a new endgame boss
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Enemies:</span>
      - Cult Knight and Cult Werewolf added as new enemy types
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Equipment:</span>
      - New Silver Cult armor pieces (hood, tunic, gloves, pants) plus superior gloves
      - New weapons: Silver Grave Sword (and superior) and Mogruith Crossbow (including superior versions)

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Gameplay</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Salvaging & Economy:</span>
      - Devil Cards can be recycled into Card Scraps at the Salvager's Workbench
      - Card Scraps can be exchanged for Booster Packs or sold for Dark Coins at the Devil Merchant
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Crafting:</span>
      - New materials Dark Silver Ingot and Bolt of Dark Silver Cloth are used to craft cultist armor

<span style="color:#FFFF55">User Interface</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Boss UI:</span>
      - Bosses now show a health bar at the top of the screen
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Merchants:</span>
      - The Devil Merchant has a new multi-tab interface for easier trading

Note: The new boss and the salvage/merchant features are fresh additions and may have rough edges.

Thanks to <span style="color:#55FFFF">MAJOR76</span> for the additions!
