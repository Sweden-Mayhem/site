# Service Update

Update <span style="color:#55FF55">1.8.18</span> is live — small improvements, language support, and a few important fixes!

<span style="color:#FFFF55">New</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">User Interface:</span>
      - The Information Board now supports 9 languages. Help, Rules, Server Info, Team and Search pages will follow your chosen language.
      - NPC info screens and in-game info menus also follow your language choice.
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">HUD:</span>
      - Skill HUD now shows progress bars so you can see your skill progress at a glance.
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Books & Mail:</span>
      - A lectern has been added so you can showcase and copy books.
      - Mail tooltips clarified and you can now send empty books if you want to.

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Bug Fixes</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Combat:</span>
      - Parrying now works properly with more enchantments, and there is a new BETA visual and sound cue when an attack is incoming. Note: this cue is experimental and may change.
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Shops & Economy:</span>
      - Player shops and auction chests now correctly remove items you sell, preventing sold items from sticking around.
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Monsters:</span>
      - Fixed an issue that could cause some enemy groups to lose their correct tier labels or revert in certain areas.
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Books & Mail:</span>
      - Fixed a crash when sending mail with a full inventory.
      - Mailbox crafting now works as intended.
