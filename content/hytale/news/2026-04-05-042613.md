# Service Update

Version <span style="color:#55FF55">1.8.19</span> brings fresh fixes and improvements!

<span style="color:#FFFF55">User Interface</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Information Board:</span>
      - Information Board pages (Help, Rules, Server Info, Team, Search) now follow your chosen language - 9 languages supported
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Skill HUD:</span>
      - Skill HUD now shows progress bars so you can see skill progress in real time
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Nameplates:</span>
      - Nameplates are more responsive, no longer linger on dead entities, and perform better in busy areas

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Gameplay</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Combat:</span>
      - Parrying now works correctly with enchanted weapons and gear
      - New BETA visual and sound cues for incoming attacks to help time perfect parries
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Mobs:</span>
      - Enemy variants and their loot now load and spawn correctly, restoring the expected variety and drops
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Books & Mail:</span>
      - Mailbox recipe is fixed and available again
      - A lectern has been added so you can showcase and copy books
      - Sending mail no longer causes a crash with a full inventory; send mail tooltip clarified and empty books can be sent
      - Books now handle more languages and non-ASCII characters better

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Bug Fixes</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Shops & Auction:</span>
      - Player-run shops now correctly remove sold items from chests so sold items won't remain behind

Heads up - the new incoming-attack VFX/SFX cue is a BETA feature and may have rough edges.
