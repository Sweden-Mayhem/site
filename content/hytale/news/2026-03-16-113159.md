# Service Update

Version <span style="color:#55FF55">1.7.73</span> brings updates to the Information Board, NPCs, shops and lootboxes!

<span style="color:#FFFF55">New</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Language:</span>
      - The Information Board and Help pages now respect your chosen language, so menus and notices will appear in your preferred language (new feature, may have rough edges).

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Bug Fixes</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Information Board:</span>
      - Announcements now display with correct layout and padding
      - Information Board is more stable when joining or leaving
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">NPCs:</span>
      - NPCs no longer duplicate or spawn twice
      - Interacting with NPCs now opens their pages and dialogues reliably
      - Cloned or copied NPCs now behave correctly after being created
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Shops & Marketplace:</span>
      - Daily purchase limits in shops now reset properly each day
      - Player-run shops and the Town House auctioneer now save and behave more reliably
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Stability & Crashes:</span>
      - Opening lootboxes should no longer cause crashes
      - Visiting areas with special animated models is far less likely to crash your game

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Gameplay</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Showcases:</span>
      - Some display/showcase blocks are now breakable where intended

Thanks to <span style="color:#55FFFF">KyuubiDDragon</span> for the improvements!
