# Service Update

Version <span style="color:#55FF55">1.8.24</span> brings polish to the Information Board!

<span style="color:#FFFF55">User Interface</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Information Board:</span>
      - Information Board content now scrolls when it gets long, so sections and announcements won't spill off-screen
      - Section buttons are more compact, letting more items fit on the screen for a cleaner layout
      - Improved visuals: nicer card backgrounds, hover effects, and icons that match the game's inventory look
      - Sections now show live counts, for example how many rules or announcements are listed
      - Single-row buttons no longer stretch awkwardly across the screen
      - A personalized "Welcome back, <your name>" greeting now appears on the Information Board
      - The Information Board can now display a banner/logo at the top
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Localization:</span>
      - Language support and translations have been updated across the board

Note: the new banner and personalized greeting are new additions and may still have small issues

Thanks to <span style="color:#55FFFF">KyuubiDDragon</span> for the improvements!
