# Service Update

Version <span style="color:#55FF55">1.8.12</span> brings some neat fixes and improvements!

<span style="color:#FFFF55">User Interface</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Information Board:</span>
      - Help, Rules, Server Info, Team and Search pages on the Information Board now follow your selected language
      - Language selection now applies consistently across the server info UI, fixing pages that sometimes stayed in the wrong language
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Nameplates:</span>
      - Entity nameplates display health and other info more reliably
      - Vanilla NPCs now get nameplates correctly
      - Nameplate preview and scrolling behavior improved for a smoother UI experience

<span style="color:#FFFF55">Gameplay</span>
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Shops:</span>
      - Barter shops now correctly remove items from the shop chest when those items are sold
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Mobs & Loot:</span>
      - Elite enemies now drop their expected vanilla loot again (for example fabric scraps and bone fragments) alongside their special drops
   <span style="color:#FFAA00">Comet Raids:</span>
      - Most comet raid themes can now spawn naturally in the world more reliably
      - Reward chests are placed more reliably so they do not overlap themed assets, and their front now faces north for consistent pickup access
      - You can no longer re-trigger a raid wave while its reward chest still exists, preventing accidental double starts

Multi-language support is a major addition here; try switching languages and let us know if you spot anything odd.
